### Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist





&#x20;1) Use strong and unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).



&#x20;2) Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone.



&#x20;3) Verify links, emails, and messages before clicking to avoid phishing scams.



&#x20;4) Keep devices updated with the latest security updates.



&#x20;5) Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive activities.



&#x20;6) (UPI Safety) Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; it is only required to send money.



&#x20;7) (UPI Safety) Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve requests from unknown users.



&#x20;8) Install apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.



&#x20;9) Regularly check bank transactions for any suspicious activity.



&#x20;10) Report cyber fraud on National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call 1930.
