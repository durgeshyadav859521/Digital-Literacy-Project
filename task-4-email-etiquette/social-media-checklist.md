#### Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist (For College Students)



##### Do’s 



&#x20;1) Maintain a professional profile with appropriate photo and bio.

&#x20;2) Think before posting and ensure respectful content.

&#x20;3) Use privacy settings to protect personal information.

&#x20;4) Share useful academic or skill-based content.

&#x20;5) Communicate politely and respect others’ opinions.





##### Don’ts 



&#x20;1) Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.

&#x20;2) Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.

&#x20;3) Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling.

&#x20;4) Avoid spreading fake or unverified information.

&#x20;5) Do not waste excessive time on social media during study hours.
